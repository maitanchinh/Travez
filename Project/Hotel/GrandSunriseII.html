Describe

Grand Sunrise 2 Hotel near beautiful My Khe Beach - one of the most favorable locations of Da Nang city. Here, you only take less than 3 minutes to walk to the famous My Khe beach, and 10 minutes on the car to move from Danang International Airport to the hotel. Grand Sunrise 2 is located in the middle of the intersection of attractive tourist sites like Ba Na, Hoi An Ancient Town, Co Vien Cham, etc. In addition, visitors also have the opportunity to enjoy the famous bridges of Da Nang, especially the Dragon Bridge and Tran Thi Ly Bridge. Designed in the style of interference between two Eurasian architectural styles breaking the way, modern facilities and equipment, monthly space and professional and friendly staff. Grand Sunrise 2 Hotel is considered an ideal resort when visitors come to Da Nang. Grand Sunrise 3-star standard hotel always wants to affirm its brand and position in the heart of domestic and foreign tourists.

Speciality

Located in Da Nang, Grand Sunrise 2 Hotel is 300 meters from My Khe Beach. The hotel has a terrace and an outdoor pool as well as an on-site bar.
All rooms are equipped with cable TV, a kettle and a private bathroom with slippers and free toiletries, for guests' comfort. Grand Sunrise 2 Hotel features free Wi-Fi.
The hotel provides 24-hour front desk service.
Grand Sunrise 2 Hotel is 1.9 km from Da Nang Love Bridge and 2.4 km from Cham Museum. The nearest airport is Da Nang International Airport, 5 km from the property.
Couples especially like this place - they give 8.3 points for a holiday for 2 people.
